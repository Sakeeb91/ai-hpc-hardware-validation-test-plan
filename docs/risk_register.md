## Guidance

- Use concrete failure domains from `failure_classification.md`.
- Link risks to evidence, issue reports, or blocked matrix rows.
- Assign an owner and status for every open risk.
- Do not use the risk register to hide missing validation; missing required evidence should remain visible as `BLOCKED` or `PASS WITH WARNINGS`.
